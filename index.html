About

My interests sit at the intersection of restoration, aquatic ecology, field observation, and environmental planning. I’m especially interested in coastal wetlands, fisheries ecology, habitat monitoring, and conservation work that connects science with place.

Research & Field Interests

Aquatic Ecology

Juvenile alosine diet analysis, anadromous fish habitat, and biological sample processing.

Restoration

Salt marsh restoration, coastal habitat monitoring, wildlife surveys, and refuge-based fieldwork.

Planning

Environmental planning, conservation policy, climate adaptation, and community-scale resilience.
