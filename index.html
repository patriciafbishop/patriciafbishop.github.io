Environmental Planning · Ecology Research · Fieldwork

I’m a recent graduate of environmental planning and policy, ecology researcher, and artist based in Portland, Maine. My work is rooted in curiosity about wetlands, rivers, coastal habitats, and the species that move through them. I’m especially interested in restoration, fisheries ecology, habitat monitoring, and the ways science can be communicated through both research and visual storytelling.
